DaySeven · Notification Playbook

What every user receives, by lifecycle stage.
All notifications fire in the user's local time. · v1.5.7

① Downloaded · no account

Unreachable — no push token. Only the in-app paywall reaches them.

② Locked out · signed up, never subscribed

Engagement is silenced. Win-back is the only sequence — day 1 / 3 / 7, 6 PM local:
Day 1"Your free week is waiting 🎁" — start your 7-day free trial
Day 3"Your goals are ready 💪"
Day 7"Last call 👋"

③ Trialing

Activation"Start your first streak 🔥" (~day 1) · "You're building something 💪" (~day 3) — week 1 only
Trial-end"Your free week ends tomorrow ⏳" (~24h) · "Last chance — trial ending ⏰" (~2h) — only if they cancelled auto-renew
Engagementdaily 9 AM · streak 8 PM · goal Thu 8 AM · weekly Sun 10 AM · monthly 1st 10 AM
Alsostreak milestones · goals complete · new-workout detected · friend & social · challenges

④ Paying subscriber

The full engagement set above — daily, streak, goal, weekly/monthly summaries, milestones, social, challenges. No activation, trial-end, or win-back (they self-exclude once a user has access).

⑤ Lapsed · trial or subscription expired

Engagement is silenced. Win-back only, clocked from the lapse date — day 1 / 3 / 7, 6 PM local:
Has progress"We saved your spot 👋" — resubscribe, leans on their streak & stats
Never engaged"Your plan is still here 🎯" — resubscribe, leans on their plan (no false "your stats" claim)
Grandfathered users keep access for life → they never land here; they get the paying set.
