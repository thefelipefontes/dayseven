Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2025

DaySeven ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (if signing up with email)
  • Display name and profile photo
  • Authentication data from Apple or Google (if using social sign-in)
  • Push notification tokens (for sending reminders and updates)

Fitness Data

  • Workout logs (type, duration, date)
  • Weekly goals and streak information
  • Progress photos (stored securely and only visible to you unless shared)
  • Activity statistics and personal records
  • Health data from Apple HealthKit (steps, calories, workouts, heart rate) — read only, never shared with third parties or used for advertising

Social Features

  • Friends list and friend requests
  • Activity feed posts
  • Reactions to friends' activities
  • Leaderboard rankings

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • General location (country/region) for analytics

Purchase Information

  • Subscription status and entitlements (managed through RevenueCat)
  • We do not collect or store payment details — all transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Track your workouts and calculate streaks
  • Display your activity to friends (based on your privacy settings)
  • Show leaderboard rankings
  • Send push notifications (daily reminders, friend requests, streaks)
  • Send password reset emails (if using email authentication)
  • Manage your subscription and entitlements
  • Improve the App and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests

3. How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We do NOT share Apple HealthKit data with third parties, use it for advertising, or use it for purposes other than providing health and fitness functionality within the App.

We may share information:

  • With other users: Your display name, profile photo, and activities are visible to friends based on your privacy settings
  • With service providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and storage, and RevenueCat for subscription management
  • For legal reasons: If required by law or to protect our rights

4. Your Privacy Controls

You can control your privacy in the App:

  • Show/hide your activity from the friends feed
  • Show/hide your profile from leaderboards
  • Delete your account and all associated data

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Access controls

Apple HealthKit data is read directly from your device and is not stored on our servers beyond what is necessary to display your activity within the App.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

DaySeven is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@dayseven.app

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what data we collect and the right to request deletion.

11. International Users

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located.
